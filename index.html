Questions, answered

Is it really free?

Yes — the reader and all browser tools are free with no limits on file count. Paid extras are optional: AI plans from ₹49 and premium server tools past their daily free 50 pages.

Do my files get uploaded?

Not for the free tools — they run entirely in your browser. A few ★ premium tools use our server and always ask for your consent first, then delete everything after the job.

Do I need an account?

No. Use the tools and guest reading without one. An account unlocks the AI companion and the wallet.

What formats are supported?

PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Excel, images, text, Markdown and webpages. See all tools.
