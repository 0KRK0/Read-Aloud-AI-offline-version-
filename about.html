About · Lexora AI

Your AI companion for every document

Lexora AI is an independent, privacy-first document app made in India.

What it is

Lexora AI turns any document into something you can listen to, talk to and work on. The reader speaks your PDFs aloud with karaoke highlighting. The AI companion explains anything you select, summarizes pages, and answers questions about the document. And a full suite of 40+ tools — merge, split, compress, convert, OCR, sign, edit, redact, crop, translate — handles the everyday document work.

Why it's different

Almost everything runs in your browser. Your files are not uploaded to be merged, compressed or signed — they never leave your device. Where a real server genuinely helps (HD conversions, whole-document research), Lexora asks for your consent first, shows the exact price, and deletes everything afterwards. Privacy isn't a setting here; it's the default architecture.

Made in India

Lexora AI is built in India, prices in rupees, and believes powerful document tools should cost a fraction of what the big suites charge — most of it is simply free.
